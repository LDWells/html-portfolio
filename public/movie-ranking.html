The Best Movies According to LaDarion

My top 3 DreamWork movies of all-time

Kung Fu Panda

An animated action-comedy film series that follows the adventures of Po, a clumsy and lovable giant panda, as he becomes the Dragon Warrior, the legendary protector of the Valley of Peace.

Over The Hedge

An animated comedy film released in 2006, based on the comic strip of the same name by Michael Fry and T. Lewis. The movie follows a group of woodland creatures as they venture into the suburban neighborhood beyond their hedge to find food and face the challenges of human encroachment on their habitat.

Shrek

The series follows the adventures of Shrek, an ogre with a heart of gold, as he navigates through a whimsical fairy tale world filled with quirky characters and unconventional love stories.
